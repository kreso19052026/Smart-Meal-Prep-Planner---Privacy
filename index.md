Smart Meal Planner stores all user data (meal plans, recipes, shopping
lists) locally on the device using SQLite. No personal data is collected
or transmitted to external servers.

The app uses Google AdMob for advertising; AdMob's data practices are
governed by Google's Privacy Policy (https://policies.google.com/privacy).
Internet permission is required solely for loading advertisements.
